My plan for next year:

Next year I'm planning to go to university, probably NMU or Emeris and study software engineering/ software development. I'm not choosing because it sounds good (even though that is a bonus!) but because it's a passion of mine. I don't really enjoy pure theory-heavy stuff I'm more into building things, solving problems and having the pleasure of seeing the final product work.

I already spend my time doing web development (a branch of software engineering) messing around with code, building small systems and figuring things out for myself. So for me this field makes sense because I want to grow this skill properly into an actual job or create my own projects that can be used in real life.

I'm not trying to do this just for a Bachealor Degree (even though that is part of it!) I am doing this so I can spend the rest of my life doing something I love.
