Practical Skills Task research

This page shows research on the 8 most important skills (soft skill and hard skill) that will support me next year. I will descibe how and why they will be useful for life after school.

Approach

Style Analysis

We looked at modern portfolio and agency websites to understand what makes them feel clean, how they guide attention, how spacing affects perception, and why simple layouts usually look more professional. We didn’t copy anything — we just studied what actually works.

Visual Structure Rebuild

This was the main change. We rebuilt the layout so it actually flows: sections follow a clear order, spacing is more consistent, alignment is cleaner throughout, and unnecessary clutter was removed. The goal was clarity above everything else.

Typography Refinement

Typography was simplified and structured properly with clearer hierarchy, consistent font usage, better spacing between text sections, and easier scanability. Small changes, but a big difference in readability.

Inspiration-Based Reconstruction

The team took inspiration from high-end portfolio and agency sites, but rebuilt everything in our own way. The focus was clean structure, strong hierarchy, and minimal but intentional design — not copying, just understanding what works and applying it properly.
